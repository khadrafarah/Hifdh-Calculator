How This Works ▾
① Set your daily goal — choose how many pages you want to memorise per day and how many days a week you'll sit down to memorise. Start small; consistency matters more than speed.
② Tell us your starting point — enter how many pages you've already memorised, or pick by Juz or Surah. The calculator works out exactly how many pages remain.
③ Gradual progressive increase — with this toggle on, your daily page target and days per week increase gradually across fixed phases, preventing burnout while building steady momentum.
④ Hit Calculate — you'll see your estimated completion date, a breakdown of how long each phase takes, and how many juz remain.
Tip: Use the Progress tab to tick off each page as you memorise it, and the Preparation tab to read ahead 2 weeks in advance — this is one of the most effective techniques for easier memorisation.
